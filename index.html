What We Offer

American Care specializes in providing online doctor consultations for quick and convenient diagnosis and treatment of common health issues.

1) Register

Complete a secure, private health profile and proceed to your free webcam consultation with a trained medical provider.

2) Online Visit

Speak with a recommended healthcare professional via webcam, phone, or app at a date and time most convenient to you.

3) Treatment

Recieve a diagnosis and treatment plan from your American Care doctor or nurse practitioner. If needed, you'll be provided with a prescription sent to a local pharmacy for pick-up.

Additional Services

In addition to our world class telemedical service, we offer additional solutions for a variety of online medical needs.

Pediatrics
Connect parents and their children to pediatric healthcare providers from school, home, or anywhere else. School nurses may also utilize our pediatric services when a student suffers from more serious symptoms.

Mental Health
Instantly connect with on-call mental health specialists for consolation in a crisis. Alert emergency personnel if needed.

Chronic Conditions
Offer patients with lifelong conditions urgent and follow-up care as needed. Keep records of medical history and progression of symptoms for future reference.
